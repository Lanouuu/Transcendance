When we used the Zaquel armillary sphere prototype, everything disappeared: the lab, the sphere and my team, except for my assistant, Iris, and the quartz. The sphere was miscalibrated on the fundamental network of events. We've been transported to a parallel node of the network.

We encountered a gothic fox ghost named Irkalla. Completely obnoxious, but Iris likes him. The quartz crystal has a strange effect on him.

Iris showed him some games on her BlackBerry. Why the fuck are ghosts so obsessed by video games ?

I found a way to escape this parallel node. The quartz makes Irkalla uneasy because he's linked to the network by his inner chaos - the quartz is too ordered, they are poles apart. By exciting him enough, he might be able to send us home.

It worked ! Iris managed to get out. I'm about to do the same. Dear future explorers, find Irkalla and give him video games. He's your only way out.
